Data Domains (12 Categories)

1. Demographics & Population

American Community Survey, Data Commons timeseries. Annual estimates at national to block-group level.

2. Economics & Finance

GDP, CPI, unemployment, retail sales, mortgage rates from BLS, Fed, Treasury, IMF, World Bank, OECD, ECB, BIS.

3. Capital Markets

Daily stock prices (OHLCV), FX rates, SEC filings (10-Q/10-K metrics, NPORT, Form ADV).

4. Banking & Financial Institutions

Bank financials, FDIC deposits, parent-subsidiary hierarchies, call reports.

5. Real Estate & Housing

FHFA house price indexes, mortgage performance, appraisals, Freddie Mac HPI, construction permits, homelessness.

6. Weather & Climate

NOAA daily global data (80k+ stations), METAR/TAF airport weather, NWS observations, water supply.

7. Energy & Environment

EIA energy stats, EPA emissions, GHG by sector (Climate Watch, EDGAR, OWID), nuclear reactors.

8. Crime & Safety

FBI national/state crime estimates. Urban crime by city, zip, offense category.

9. Transportation

US DOT domestic air segment data (non-stop routes, carriers, passengers).

10. Labor & Employment

ILO global labor stats, weekly unemployment claims, WARN Act layoff notices.

11. Tax & Migration

IRS income stats, migration flows (county-to-county), Form 990 nonprofits, Economic Census.

12. International Trade

WTO trade flows, USDA ag commodities, UNIDO manufacturing, Canadian/UK statistics.

Standard Timeseries Pattern

SELECT g.GEO_NAME, g.LEVEL, t.VARIABLE_NAME, t.DATE, t.VALUE, t.UNIT
FROM <ANY_TIMESERIES_TABLE> t
JOIN GEOGRAPHY_INDEX g ON t.GEO_ID = g.GEO_ID
WHERE <filters>
ORDER BY t.DATE DESC;

Variable Decode Pattern

SELECT t.*, a.MEASURE, a.FREQUENCY, a.UNIT
FROM <TIMESERIES> t
JOIN <MATCHING_ATTRIBUTES> a ON t.VARIABLE = a.VARIABLE
WHERE <filters>;

Quick Start SQL Queries

1. Browse the Data Catalog

SELECT TABLE_NAME, TABLE_TYPE, SHORT_DESCRIPTION
FROM SNOWFLAKE_PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.PUBLIC_DATA_CATALOG
ORDER BY TABLE_TYPE, TABLE_NAME;

2. Search for Specific Variables

SELECT TABLE_NAME, VARIABLE_NAME, UNIT, FREQUENCY, MIN_DATE, MAX_DATE
FROM SNOWFLAKE_PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.PUBLIC_DATA_VARIABLE_CATALOG
WHERE VARIABLE_NAME ILIKE '%GDP%'
LIMIT 20;

3. US GDP with Geography Names

SELECT g.GEO_NAME, t.VARIABLE_NAME, t.DATE, t.VALUE, t.UNIT
FROM SNOWFLAKE_PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.FINANCIAL_ECONOMIC_INDICATORS_TIMESERIES t
JOIN SNOWFLAKE_PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.GEOGRAPHY_INDEX g ON t.GEO_ID = g.GEO_ID
WHERE t.VARIABLE_NAME ILIKE '%Real Gross Domestic Product%'
  AND g.GEO_NAME = 'United States'
ORDER BY t.DATE DESC
LIMIT 20;

4. Company Stock Price Lookup

SELECT c.COMPANY_NAME, s.TICKER, s.VARIABLE_NAME, s.DATE, s.VALUE
FROM SNOWFLAKE_PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.COMPANY_INDEX c
JOIN SNOWFLAKE_PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.STOCK_PRICE_TIMESERIES s
  ON c.PRIMARY_TICKER = s.TICKER
WHERE c.COMPANY_NAME ILIKE '%Apple%'
  AND s.VARIABLE_NAME = 'Post-Market Close'
ORDER BY s.DATE DESC
LIMIT 20;

5. Geography Hierarchy Roll-up

SELECT p.GEO_NAME AS parent, p.LEVEL AS parent_level,
       c.GEO_NAME AS child, c.LEVEL AS child_level
FROM SNOWFLAKE_PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.GEOGRAPHY_HIERARCHY h
JOIN SNOWFLAKE_PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.GEOGRAPHY_INDEX p ON h.PARENT_GEO_ID = p.GEO_ID
JOIN SNOWFLAKE_PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.GEOGRAPHY_INDEX c ON h.GEO_ID = c.GEO_ID
WHERE p.GEO_NAME = 'Texas'
LIMIT 20;

6. Cross-Domain Analysis (Weather + Holidays)

SELECT w.DATE, g.GEO_NAME, w.VARIABLE_NAME, w.VALUE,
       h.HOLIDAY_NAME
FROM SNOWFLAKE_PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.NOAA_WEATHER_METRICS_TIMESERIES w
JOIN SNOWFLAKE_PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.GEOGRAPHY_INDEX g ON w.GEO_ID = g.GEO_ID
LEFT JOIN SNOWFLAKE_PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.PUBLIC_DATA_FREE.PUBLIC_HOLIDAY_CALENDAR h
  ON w.GEO_ID = h.GEO_ID AND w.DATE = h.DATE
WHERE g.GEO_NAME ILIKE '%Chicago%'
  AND w.DATE >= '2025-01-01'
ORDER BY w.DATE
LIMIT 20;
